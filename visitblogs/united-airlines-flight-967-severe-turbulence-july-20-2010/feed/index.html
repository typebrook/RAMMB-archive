]]> By: Physics Prof ./visitblogs/united-airlines-flight-967-severe-turbulence-july-20-2010/#comment-17 Wed, 04 Aug 2010 21:14:48 +0000 http://rammb.cira.colostate.edu/visit/blog/index.php/2010/07/29/united-airlines-flight-967-severe-turbulence-july-20-2010/#comment-17 I was on the flight. About 95% of the people on board had no noticeable injuries and sought no treatment. As far as I know and saw, all of the people that were injured were not wearing a seat belt or had it too loose.

The plane had a downward acceleration for about 0.5 s. From the way that things moved in the plane, my estimate is that the average acceleration of the plane was negative 3 g’s for 0.5 s. If you’re not wearing your seat belt, you get launched upwards at 2 g’s relative to the plane.
